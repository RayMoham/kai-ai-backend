The exam-based course in Database Systems offers an in-depth exploration of database design, implementation, and management. 
This course emphasizes theoretical understanding and assessment through exams, ensuring students grasp fundamental concepts such as data modeling, SQL, and database architecture. 
The curriculum includes rigorous training in database normalization, indexing, and optimization techniques, alongside essential skills in database administration, security, and recovery. 
Through quizzes, assignments, and a comprehensive final exam, students will demonstrate their mastery of database systems, preparing them for advanced studies and careers in data management and related fields.
